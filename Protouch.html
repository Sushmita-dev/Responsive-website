Protouch Sports

About us: Protouch began its journey as an apparel brand. From its inception, the founder Indrajit Bhalotia had a vision to become the number 1 golf solution company in India. Golf was yet to see revolution in the 90’s. It was hardly accessible and rather exclusive. But the young pro golfer was determined to be the wave of change that the country needed in terms of this elite sport. Thus, began a struggle to set things right. Protouch transpired into sectors beyond luxury apparel. Protouch became the first golf academy of Eastern India and combined the latest technological developments in sports science with the best coaching. However, Protouch was not just a revolution in terms of its extraordinary golf schooling. We extended the opportunity, to thousands of people, to be a part of the golfing fraternity. We brought golf to the masses. Protouch also offers its expertise to different golf courses via facility management. For more than 30 years, Protouch has been influencing, inspiring and rejuvenating the addiction of this beautiful sport and its effects on and course and beyond.

Academy

Protouch Golf academy understands the true essence of golf and tutors beyond golf technicalities.

Protouch Golf Academy was established in May 1990, in Kolkata. Its main objective is to give international quality golf coaching at an affordable price.

Protouch has 26 NGAI certified experienced golf coaches onboard along with a core team of sports management professionals.

Toddler Golf is a trademark of Protouch Golf Academy. Protouch golf academy also supports and trains for the underprivileged children.

RCGC Junior Program

Starting with a modest number of 20 juniors back in 2003, the RCGC Junior Golf Program has come a long way since under the guidance of Indrajit Bhalotia and his Protouch Golf Academy. The RCGC Junior Program has been an integral part in developing a base of remarkable 600 plus young golfers in the city. Scientific golf coaching, world class practice facilities, top quality training equipment, application of sports science have been the key to the success of the junior program.

CLGC Junior Program

Protouch Golf Academy aims to bring golf to the masses. Junior golfers need not be affiliated to any clubs to be a part of the program in Maidan. The main objective is to promote golf as an accessible sport in India. It is an open for all, affordable program that provides quality training. Students can learn for a fee as trivial as Rs.200 per session. One need not invest in expensive equipment either. Protouch provides free equipment and balls to the juniors.


BPGC Junior Program

The BPGC is the most prestigious golf club in Mumbai & in 2009 underwent extensive redesign & modifications to the course. It’s a very demanding course & has a large member community that is largely focused on golf. Protouch organizes extensive Junior Camps at BPGC.



Hyderabad Junior Program

The Hyderabad Golf club is located in a historic setting in and around Golconda fort. Hyderabad’s first and only public golf course, it is accessible to golfers from all over the world. The Hyderabad Golf Association, a NON PROFIT organisation was formed by a voluntary group of golfers in the year 1993 with an object to encourage, promote and develop the sport of golf, which is now an Olympic sport and establish an international standard golf course.

Thousands of potential sports superstars do not even get to the stage where they can showcase their talent – they are forced to choose academics over sports. Champ For Life (CFL) Academy has been conceptualized to enable young sportspersons to pursue their passion with obsession. With Champ for Life (CFL), you no longer have to choose between a basic education and pursuing a dream in sports (or an alternative career).

YOU CAN DO BOTH!

CFL enrolls students in NIOS (classes 10 and 12) and IGNOU (Graduation) and provides the entire tuition in a concise simple form. The course content is edited to reduce study time by almost 80 percent. Students can devote the time saved to their chosen field. Study modules are presented through summaries, Q & A and audio visual lectures. NIOS and IGNOU are both Boards recognized by the Government. In addition, CFL offers 7 complementary Subjects, specially formulated for athletes and budding professionals to prepare them for their chosen field.
